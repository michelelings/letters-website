hermana in Dutch

hermana is zus in Dutch, the everyday word for a sister.

Pronunciation

Zus sounds like “zuss” (rhymes loosely with English “bus”).

Example sentence

Mi hermana estudia medicina.

Mijn zus studeert geneeskunde.

My sister studies medicine.
